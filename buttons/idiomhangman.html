How to Play
Idiom Dash — Quick Guide
The Goal
💡 A definition is shown. Guess the English idiom that matches it — one letter at a time.
⏰ You start with 3 minutes. Solve as many idioms as you can before time runs out.
Tapping Letters
✅ Correct guess — the letter appears in the blank boxes. You earn time back and a gem bonus.
❌ Wrong guess — time is deducted. The more wrong guesses in a row, the more time you lose.
Streaks & Gems 💎
🔥 Correct run — each consecutive correct guess earns more time and gems. Keep the streak alive!
💔 Wrong run — each consecutive wrong guess costs more time. Gems are also deducted.
Clues
🏷 The theme (e.g. Courage, Money) gives you a hint about what kind of idiom it is.
📚 The definition tells you what the idiom means — use it to figure out the words.
Dashboard
▫ Open My Progress Dashboard to see which idioms you’ve attempted. Tap any card to play that idiom directly.
Each idiom is saved to your profile — impress your friends on the leaderboard! 🏆

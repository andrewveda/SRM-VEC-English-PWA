Why Mystic?

Inspired by Elon Musk’s love for The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy, Mystic Summit treats learning as exploration: clear thinking, smart choices, steady action.

How Mystic Summit runs

Each department designs a compact set of events. First-year students take full charge as organisers. The Summit unfolds over a few days, followed by reflection and refinement.

For student organisers

  • Plan the schedule and map each track to clear outcomes.
  • Take roles: convenor, coordinator, logistics, communication, tech.
  • Coordinate across sections and departments with simple tools.
  • Practise once before the event: rehearse hosting and timing.
Calm execution Ownership under pressure Clear instructions

For participants

  • Join, attempt, experiment, fail, repair and learn in short bursts.
  • Meet peers from other branches and sections.
  • Practise speaking, presenting and listening in a friendly space.
  • Share feedback that helps refine the next Summit cycle.

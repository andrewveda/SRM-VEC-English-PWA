How to Play
Lexicon — Quick Guide
The Goal
💬A dialogue is shown with the target phrase blanked out. Guess the phrase one letter at a time.
⏱You have 3 minutes. Solve as many phrases as you can before time runs out.
Clues
🏷The category tag tells you the semantic group.
📖The definition gives you the precise meaning.
💬The dialogue shows the phrase in a real conversation — with the words you need to fill in shown as blanks.
Scoring
✅Correct guess — earn time and gems. Your streak multiplies rewards.
❌Wrong guess — lose time. Water drips from above. Consecutive wrong guesses flood faster!

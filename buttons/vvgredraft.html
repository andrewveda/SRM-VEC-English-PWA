Lexicon — Quick Guide
The Goal
📚A definition is shown. Guess the GRE or IELTS word that matches it — one letter at a time.
⏰You have 3 minutes. Solve as many words as you can before time runs out.
Clues
🏷The category tells you the semantic group of the word.
📚The definition gives you the precise meaning.
Scoring
✅Correct guess — earn time and gems. Streaks multiply rewards.
❌Wrong guess — lose time. Consecutive wrong guesses cost more.
Glossary & Mastery
📚Open the Glossary (book icon) to browse all words with definitions and example dialogues.
✨Mark a word as Mastered when you know it cold. It's saved to your profile and counts as complete.
